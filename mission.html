Our Mission

Guided by purpose. Empowering youth. Transforming communities.

Our Commitment

APESE ERE is committed to creating transformative opportunities for youth across Turkana by enabling them to lead, innovate, and empower their communities. We believe in action-driven mission work.

What Drives Us

We are driven by the belief that every young person holds the power to change their world. Our mission is grounded in sustainability, equity, and social transformation.

Strategic Focus

Our mission is operationalized through community-led initiatives in agriculture, technology, education, health, and youth leadership development.
